Digital threats are real, growing, and increasingly targeting individuals,
institutions, and communities. I want to use this skill to serve and protect.
To defend those who need it, and when necessary, go on the offensive for the
right reasons, with the right intention.

For the sake of Allah, I want to be someone who can both shield and strike.

## My Goal

To build real expertise on both sides of cybersecurity:

- **Red Team** - Offensive security, ethical hacking, penetration testing
- **Blue Team** - Defense, threat detection, incident response, SOC

Not one or the other. Both.
